Our Story

Coffee is a ritual,
not a routine.

We started Mocha & Co in 2018 with one mission: make specialty
coffee accessible to everyone. Not intimidating, not pretentious — just
excellent.

We source directly from farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and
Guatemala. We roast in small batches. We pull every shot with
intention.

Why We're Different

The Experience

01

Direct Trade

We buy directly from farmers at fair prices. No middlemen. Every bag tells you exactly where it came from and who grew it.

02

Small Batch Roasting

Our roaster runs in 5kg batches, twice a week. Fresh coffee every time — never sitting on a shelf for months.

03

Trained Baristas

Every member of our team goes through a 3-month craft training program. Your cup is in good hands.
